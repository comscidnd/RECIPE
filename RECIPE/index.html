Filipino Rice Porridge / Arroz Caldo

Warm and soft rice porridge na swak sa breakfast o kapag gusto mo ng comfort food.

Equipment

  • Large Pot
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Wooden Spoon or Ladle

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice (washed)
  • 1/2 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced thin
  • 6 cups water or chicken broth
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Boiled egg, fried garlic, chopped spring onions

Procedure

  1. Saut√© garlic and ginger in a pot until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken and cook lightly.
  3. Pour in water or chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add rice and simmer, stirring occasionally, until it becomes thick and porridge-like.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot with toppings like boiled egg, fried garlic, and spring onions.

Sinigang na Baboy

Classic Filipino sour soup with pork, vegetables, and a tangy broth.

Equipment

  • Large Pot
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Wooden Spoon or Ladle

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 cup kangkong (water spinach)
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • 1 packet sinigang mix or fresh tamarind
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. In a large pot, boil pork with water until tender.
  2. Add onion and tomatoes, simmer until softened.
  3. Add radish and cook until tender.
  4. Stir in sinigang mix or fresh tamarind, adjusting sourness to taste.
  5. Add kangkong and chili peppers, cook for a few minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.

Chicken Adobo

Classic Filipino dish with chicken marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, and spices.

Equipment

  • Large Pot
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Wooden Spoon or Ladle

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Marinate chicken in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add marinade and simmer until chicken is tender and sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt to taste, then serve hot with rice.

Paksiw na Isda

Traditional Filipino dish with fish cooked in vinegar, ginger, and spices.

Equipment

  • Large Pot
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Wooden Spoon or Ladle

Ingredients

  • 1 kg fish (bangus or tilapia), cleaned and cut
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. In a pot, combine fish, vinegar, water, ginger, chili peppers, and onion.
  2. Bring to a boil, then simmer until fish is cooked through.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve hot with rice.
